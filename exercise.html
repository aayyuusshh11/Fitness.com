There are thee main components or parts of the body which are required to train for overall development of the Body which makes you look more Fit in any outfit or condition.

(1) Upper Body

An upper body workout provides numerous benefits including improved posture, decreased risk of injury, increased strength in your arms, shoulders, and back, enhanced daily activity performance, better bone density, calorie burning, and can even contribute to improved balance and coordination across your entire body


(2) Lower Body

Lower body workouts can help you build muscle, improve your posture, and strengthen your bones. They can also help you burn calories, manage stress, and improve your overall fitness.


(3) Exercises for Back

Back exercises can help improve your posture, reduce pain, and prevent injury. They can also help you breathe better, boost your energy, and improve your overall well-being.
